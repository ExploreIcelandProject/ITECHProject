Skogafoss Falls

Skógafoss is one of the biggest waterfalls in Iceland, with a drop of 60 meters and a width of 25 meters, and you can walk right up to, but be prepared to be drenched. It is just overwhelming standing next to it.

Seeing that it produces a lot of drizzle then you will be able to see beautiful rainbows on sunny days.

How to get there

Skógafoss waterfall is a prominent waterfall by route 1 and marks the beginning of Fimmvörðuháls hike over to Þórsmörk. This waterfall is seen from the Ring Road and is featured in every South Coast tour available.
